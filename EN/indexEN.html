what is worldworm?

WorldWorm is a project dealing with the future and development of modern human nutrition. We strive to bring both the nutritionally and tastefully superior foods of the future into the present..


Our vision for the food of the future is quality, organics, balanced taste and nutrition. And that's what our spreads deliver. Thanks to our long-tested recipes, healthy and nutritious worms and our youthful motivation. Our spreads contain essential and highly digestible proteins, amino acids, fatty acids, vitamins, minerals and omega fats. At the same time, they taste and smell so inviting that even a very conservative eater will overcome the block in eating insects.

our team

We met in elementary school, where the three of us became inseparable. Over time, our meetings began to move away from the classic boy-games and video-games and towards studying money and society. The first projects and business plans were coming. In 2021, we participated in a student entrepreneurship competition called Soutěž & Podnikej, to which Radoslav contributed an idea of processing insects into a more complex product. After a few test recipes, we came to the decision that a form of spread would be best and that we could manage it even with our limited cooking skills. Over time our friendly meetings turned into meetings, but mostly in tea rooms or kitchens and by no means became any more boring. Perhaps our close friendship with its intense humour is often a hindrance to any attempt at consistent work. But we are not worried about that. And so the three friends got to the worm spreads.
